About FOPPRF

FOPPRF is a cloud-based instant messaging app that allows users to send text messages, voice messages, and multimedia files. It was launched in 2013 by brothers Nikolai and Pavel Durov.

Features

  • End-to-end encryption for secure communication
  • Supports group chats with up to 200,000 members
  • Large file sharing (up to 2 GB per file)
  • Customizable themes and stickers
